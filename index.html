    About Us

    Learning Tree Edusolutions brings together Principals, Vice Principals, Heads of Departments, Educators, Academicians, experienced Business Studies and Accountancy school teachers, and CBSE Paper Setters to develop focused and high-quality resources for senior secondary school students.

    Through our flagship series, we are committed to publishing comprehensive books in Business Studies and Accountancy aligned with various examinations. At present, our publications include Objective Type Questions in Business Studies (Class XII) and Textbook on Business Studies (Class XII), published by Goyal Brothers Prakashan.

    We work closely with experienced school teachers and subject experts from schools of high repute to ensure that our content remains practical, classroom-tested, student-friendly, and up-to-date with the latest guidelines of respective academic bodies.
